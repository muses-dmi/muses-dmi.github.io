Muses - Digital Musical Instruments

2019-05-10

Muses is an umbrella project for work being done in the Physical Computing Group at University of West of England (UWE). Our particular focus is applying programming language theory and practice, physical computing, networking, and musicial theory to design and implementation of Digitial Musical Instruments (DMI).
